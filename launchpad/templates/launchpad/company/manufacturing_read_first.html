Get started with Assembly / Manufacturing

Aerobridge gives you the ability manage assembly and manufacturing of drone and its components with a four step process



  1. Start with adding master components, master components are the core building blocks that form the drone assembly.
  2. You can then create an Aircraft Model by linking master components to a Aircraft Model.
  3. Once master components are added, you can acknowledge custody of components to add components to Aerobridge.
  4. You can then create a Aircraft Assembly based on acknolwedged components.
