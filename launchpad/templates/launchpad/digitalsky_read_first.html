Get started with Digital Sky

Aerobridge is a management server for Operators and Manufacturers. It will help you with mangaging operations, keys and other operational details. It can also interact with DigitalSky, however, as of March 2021, DigitalSky is changing. Therefore, there are somethings that Aerobridge can and cannot do with respect to DigitalSky:

  • Request and get Permission Artefacts
  • Securely store keys and flight plans
  • Upload signed logs to Digital Sky

Before you begin with making Digital Sky Commands, you need to have the following, for more information, see the RPAS Guidance Manual:

  • Register as a Operator with the Digital Sky
  • Get a Unique Indentification Number (UIN) for your aircraft, see the application flow
  • Get a Unmanned Aircraft Renewal permit (UAOP), application flow
